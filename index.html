SAVED PEOPLE SERVE PEOPLE

God's plan for the "church" is to IMPACT it's community for His fame. That is why we are so excited about Sunday, September 30th! We aren't having church, we are BEING the church! We have lined up a few service/ministry opportunities, shown below, that you can sign up for to serve.


For BE THE CHURCH Sunday, we will gather at 8:30AM to worship and disperse to our projects. Your team leader will provide full details. Projects will generally conclude around 12:30pm. When projects conclude, there will be an optional lunch gathering with your serving team.

 

Looking for a Serving Opportunity?

SERVE WITH YOUR COMMUNITY GROUP

FAQ/Details

  • Will we have normal church services on Sunday, September 30th?

    No, we will not gather at Greenbrier Middle School. Normal services will resume the following Sunday at 10:10am.

  • What will the morning look like?

    Instead of gathering at Greenbrier Middle School, we will first gather at the church offices in Lenox Plaza at 8:30am. At our offices we will get final details, gather together with our teams and drive to our location. Projects will last 2.5-3 hours, concluding around 12:30pm. Teams will optionally gather for lunch after serving together. Church Office Directions

  • What about childcare?

    If you need childcare, sign-up for it on your Be The Church registration. After we gather together at the church office, you will drop your child off at the childcare home location. From there, you will go to your service project.

  • How do I need to prepare for my project? What will we be doing?

    Final project details will be communicated as soon as your team leader receives confirmation. Your team leader will communicate the details of your project to you via email. We will try to get word out as soon as possible. Just be flexible!
