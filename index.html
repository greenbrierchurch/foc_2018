SAVED PEOPLE SERVE PEOPLE

God's plan for the "church" is to IMPACT it's community for His fame. That is why we are so excited about Sunday, September 30th! We aren't having church, we are BEING the church! We have lined up a few service/ministry opportunities, shown below, that you can sign up for to serve.


For BE THE CHURCH Sunday, we will gather at 9:10AM to worship and disperse to our projects. Projects will generally conclude around noon. Your team leader will provide full details.

FAQ's/INFO

 

  • Who is AMPED for?

    Lucky kids who are entering Pre-K4 to 5th Grade or have completed the 5th grade! .

  • What is the schedule for the day?

    Each day will be full of activities, games and lessons that are designed so your kids can discover how the Creator of all things created us to know and love Him. Each day will be from 6:30-8:30PM.

    Day 1 Theme - 7/15 God is with you. Parents are invited to join us for the Day 1 Kick-off party. Pizza will be served!
    Day 2 Theme - 7/16 God has a plan for you.
    Day 3 Theme - 7/17 Jesus is alive!
    Day 4 Theme - 7/18 You are part of a bigger story.

  • Does this cost anything?

    Nope. Completely Free!

  • Will food/dinner be served?

    Dinner will only be served at the Day 1 kick off party. Snacks will be provided each day.

  • Do I need to sign up?

    Yes. The deadline to RSVP will be Monday, July 9th.

  • I would like to volunteer, what do I need to do?

    Be sure to choose the "Volunteer" option when you RSVP and we will get connected with you.

    There will be a volunteer training on Monday, June 26th at our office in Lenox Plaza. Training will be 6-8:30PM & will include dinner. Please RSVP by Wednesday, June 21st!
